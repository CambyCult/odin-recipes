Scrambled Eggs

Classic Scrambled Eggs

  • Scrambled Eggs
  • Butter
  • Parsley
  1. Heat skillet to high.
  2. introduce tablespoon of butter.
  3. Crack four fresh eggs into pan.
  4. At this point - remove skillet from heat.
  5. Reintroduce skillet to range if eggs do not continue to cook.
  6. Move eggs constantly within skillet to cook evenly. Finished eggs should have a glistening appearance.
  7. Adorn with Parsley and enjoy!
